7 Minute Workout

What is it?

High intensity interval workout, based on studies by the American College of Sports Medicine.

No special equipment required, other than a chair or surface to use for step ups and tricep dips.

Do each exercise for 30 seconds, then rest for 10 seconds. Each round lasts 7 minutes.

3 rounds are recommended.

Good luck!
